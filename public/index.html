Hart-eSports

HartCode eSports offers several different leagues. These leagues change based upon the different seasons. Currently we have the following leagues available: WoW Spring EU Cup, WoW Spring NA Cup, WoW Spring Global Cup, World of Warcraft Arena, Overwatch, Call of Duty, League of Legends NA Qualifiers, League of Legends EU Qualifiers, Dota 2 International. If you want to find more indepth rules about each league, please visit the About Me section. There you can find out all of the rules and regulaitons of each league.

Why is eSports important?

As you can see, year over year eSports Audience Growth has had a termendous increase. In 2019 the eSports Audience Growth increased to over 454 million. This is a 17.92% increase from 385 million in 2018. It is expected to keep up this increase to over 518 million in 2020, a 14.1% increase.

According to Tencent, 128million people watched the League of Legends(LoL) invitaitonal in 2018. 60 million people watched PUBG Global Invitaitonal, and only 103 million people watch the Super Bowl. Only 28 million people watched the MLB World Series 2017 (Game 7) and 24 million viewers for NBA Finals 2017 (Game 5).

Esports will bring in more than $1 billion in revenue worldwide during 2019, according to a recent report from the Newzoo analyst group. That would be a 26.4% boost from 2018, with the growth largely driven by media rights deals, sponsorships, and advertising sales.
